SPICY MISO
RAMEN BOMB

A flavor explosion that takes 45 minutes but tastes like it took 45 hours. High-velocity comfort food for the bold.

schedule 45 MINS TOTAL TIME
group SERVES 4 PORTIONS

DO THIS

01

PREP THE BASE

Whisk the miso paste, tahini, and chili oil in the bottom of each serving bowl. Don't skimp on the oil—fat is flavor, and flavor is king. Set aside and let the aromas mingle.

02

SEAR THE PORK

Get a heavy skillet screaming hot. Sear the pork belly until the edges are crispy and charred. Use the rendered fat to fry your garlic slices until they turn golden brown.

03

BOIL THE NOODLES

Bring a large pot of water to a rolling boil. Cook the ramen noodles for exactly 3 minutes. No more, no less. Al dente is the only way forward. Drain immediately.

04

ASSEMBLY BOMB

Pour hot broth into the bowls with the paste. Stir vigorously. Drop the noodles in, top with pork, the 6-minute egg, and a massive handful of spring onions.

star

PRO TIP

If you want it spicier, add a tablespoon of gochujang to the base. It adds a fermented funk that cuts through the rich pork fat perfectly.
